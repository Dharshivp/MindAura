MindAura

MindAura is an AI-powered mental wellness platform designed exclusively for higher education students. It analyzes emotional inputs in real time, detects stress levels, tracks mood patterns and ensures timely psychological support.

Get Started

🌿 What is MindAura?

MindAura is a smart digital mental health assistant that continuously monitors students’ emotional well-being using Natural Language Processing and AI models. It understands feelings expressed through text, recognizes early signs of anxiety, depression or burnout and provides immediate support resources while keeping data secure.

🤖 What Does It Do?

The system processes emotional inputs, performs sentiment analysis, classifies stress levels, generates personalized coping strategies and automatically alerts counselors in critical situations. It also maintains emotional trend reports to help institutions understand student wellness patterns.

💙 How It Helps Students

MindAura promotes emotional awareness, reduces academic stress, improves focus and prevents mental health crises by providing early interventions. Students receive real-time support, self-care techniques, motivational insights and professional help when needed — creating a healthier learning environment.
